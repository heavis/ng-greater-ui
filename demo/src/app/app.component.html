Dropdowns for range

Drop down
nggu-dropdown-range is used for setting number range based on dropdown style. it supplied two attributes, dataSource and valuesChanged.
dataSource is a instance of RangeContext class, definition as below:
# name: string; min?: number; max?: number; val1?: any; val2?: any; # 'name' will display as header, 'min' and 'max' attributes limited setting range, 'val1' and 'val2' are always contained in 'min' and 'max' range. You can gain setting result from 'val1' and 'val2'. 'valuesChanged' is a type of Event, you can attach a listener to 'valuesChanged' and the registered listener will be triggered if 'val1' or 'val2' is updated.
